saving nmap scans:
    nmap 127.0.0.1 -o nmap.txt
    nmap 127.0.0.1 | tee ./nmap.txt
    nmap 127.0.0.1 >> nmap.txt
speeding up nmap scans:
    nmap -T5 127.0.0.1 (super aggressive)
    nmap -T4 127.0.0.1 (aggressive)
    nmap -T3 127.0.0.1 (default)
scanning ports:
    nmap -p80 127.0.0.1 (port 80)
    nmap --top-ports 7 127.0.0.1 (top 7 ports)
    nmap -F 127.0.0.1 (top 100 ports)
    nmap -sT 127.0.0.1 (general ports)
